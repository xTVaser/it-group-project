Richard Stallman

Welcome, my name is Richard Stallman and I am a political advocate for free software. I personally launched the GNU project, ultimately leading to the GNU operating system, and founded the Free Software Foundation.

At the heart of my philosophy is that software should be distributed such that everyone can use and learn from it, and modify it as they see fit.
